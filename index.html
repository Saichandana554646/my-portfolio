Preventing Forgery with Blockchain powered Certificate Verification

Blockchain technology offers a robust solution for preventing certificate forgery through its decentralized, tamper-proof nature. By storing digital certificates on a blockchain, they become immutable, ensuring authenticity and preventing alteration or deletion. This decentralized approach also eliminates the need for centralized verification bodies, enhancing trust and speed.
